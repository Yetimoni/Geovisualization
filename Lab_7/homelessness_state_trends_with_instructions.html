How to Use This Chart

📋
Select a State
Use the dropdown menu at the top left of the chart to pick any US state or DC.
🖱️
Hover for Details
Move your mouse over any point on the line to see the exact homeless count for that year.
🔍
Zoom In
Click and drag on the chart to zoom into a specific time period.
↩️
Reset View
Double-click anywhere on the chart to zoom back out to the full view.
📷
Save as Image
Click the camera icon in the top right toolbar to download the chart as a PNG image.
💡 Tip: The trend line shows total homeless count from 2007 to 2024. Look for spikes around 2020 (COVID-19) and 2023-2024 (housing affordability crisis).
Data source: HUD Annual Point-in-Time Count
hudexchange.info
